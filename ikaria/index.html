Green Tea Extract

Green tea extract is a potent antioxidant that has been shown to boost metabolism and aid in fat oxidation. It contains catechins, which are compounds known to enhance weight loss by increasing the body's ability to burn fat.

Garcinia Cambogia

Garcinia cambogia is a tropical fruit that contains hydroxycitric acid (HCA), a compound that has been shown to suppress appetite and inhibit fat storage. By reducing cravings and promoting fat loss, this ingredient can support your weight management efforts.

Caffeine

Caffeine is a stimulant that can enhance energy levels and focus. It works by blocking adenosine receptors in the brain, leading to increased alertness and mental clarity. However, it is essential to note that caffeine can also have side effects, such as jitters and sleep disturbances, especially in sensitive individuals.

Forskolin

Forskolin is an extract from the roots of the Coleus forskohlii plant. It has been shown to support the breakdown of stored fat by activating an enzyme called adenylate cyclase. This process can help release stored fatty acids, making them available for energy production and weight loss.
